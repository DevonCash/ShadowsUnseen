# Nightmare at the Opera
Mysterious shadows in an opera house come to life, singing haunting arias and drawing people into the dark. Uncover the connection between the opera's past and the sinister shadows.

The ghost light at the opera house has been haunted by a stage hand after their brutal murder at the hands of an opera singer. 
The flickering shadows cast by the light are accompanied by beautiful solo operatic pieces, and anyone who investigates after midnight has their soul consumed
